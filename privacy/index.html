Privacy Policy

Builder's Eye Analytics · Last updated 24 June 2026

Builder's Eye Analytics ("the App", "we") is a personal analytics tool that lets a creator view their own social media performance in one place. This policy explains what the App accesses and how that data is handled.

1. What the App accesses

When you connect a social account (TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube) by signing in through that platform's official authorization, the App reads — on your behalf and with your consent — only your own account's:

  • basic profile information (e.g. username, display name, avatar);
  • account statistics (e.g. follower, following, like, and video/post counts);
  • your own posts/videos and their performance metrics (e.g. views, likes, comments, shares).

The App requests read-only permissions only, and accesses no one else's data.

2. How it's used

This data is used solely to display your analytics to you and to track how they change over time. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or automated decisions about other people.

3. How it's stored

Your data is stored privately on infrastructure operated by us and is accessible only to you / the operator. It is not uploaded to any third-party analytics or advertising service.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third party. The App does not post, modify, or delete anything on your connected accounts — access is read-only.

5. Third-party platforms

The App uses the official TikTok, Instagram (Meta), and YouTube (Google) APIs and abides by their platform terms. How those platforms handle your data on their own services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Revoking access & deletion

You can revoke the App's access at any time:

  • TikTok: Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions
  • Instagram / Facebook: Settings → Apps and websites
  • Google / YouTube: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party access

Revoking access stops further collection. To request deletion of stored data, contact us below.

7. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: hello@builderseye.app
