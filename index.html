Builder's Eye Analytics

A private dashboard for your own social media performance — in one place.

What it does

Builder's Eye Analytics connects to your own TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube accounts (through each platform's official, read-only authorization) and brings your performance data together so you can see what's working and improve your content. It tracks:

  • account stats — followers, following, likes, video/post counts;
  • per-video / per-post metrics — views, likes, comments, shares;
  • how these change over time.

How your data is handled

Access is read-only — nothing is posted or changed on your accounts. Your data is stored privately, shown only to you, and is never sold or shared with third parties. See the Privacy Policy for details.
